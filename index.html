ANNUAL MAINTENANCE CONTRACT

We offer annual maintenance & Preventive Maintenance (Non Comprehensive) service packages as per our customers’ needs. These services are offered at most competitive price to suit your requirements. Regular maintenance is not only mandatory as per government regulations but also essential for your own safety. AP Elevators Services is licensed elevator contractor by Government to maintain and install all types of lifts. Our AMCs package is designed to meet every customer’s needs in cost effective manner. Our trained and appropriately skilled personnel will regularly examine, lubricate and clean the machine, motor, controller, bearings, guides and if required make minor adjustments to ensure smooth and safe operation of elevator. We also provide routine checkups & emergency breakdown services along with AMCs.
